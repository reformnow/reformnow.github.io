## 1. The Disclosure Narrative: A Modern Mask for Ancient Entities
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

## 1. 揭秘叙事：古老实体的现代假面
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

With the recent surge in U.S. government "UFO disclosure," including Congressional testimony regarding "non-human biologics," the world is being primed for a paradigm shift. However, as documented in the recent discussion featuring L.A. Marzulli, what the secular world calls "Extraterrestrials" (ETs) are, in fact, the "non-human intelligences" (NHI) described in ancient Biblical texts. This is not a discovery of life from other planets, but the manifestation of the "seducing spirits and doctrines of demons" foretold in 1 Timothy 4:1.
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

随着美国政府近期“UFO揭秘”浪潮的高涨，包括有关“非人类生物制品”的国会证词，世界正被引导进入一种范式转移。然而，正如 L.A. Marzulli 在近期访谈中所记录的那样，世俗世界所谓的“外星人”（ET），实际上是古代圣经文献中所描述的“非人类智能”（NHI）。这并非发现了他星生命，而是提摩太前书 4:1 中预言的“引诱人的邪灵和鬼魔的道理”的显现。
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

## 2. Defining the Hierarchy: Angels, Fallen Angels, and Demons
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

## 2. 定义层级：天使、堕落天使与鬼魔
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

To navigate the coming deception, the Church must clearly distinguish between three distinct categories of spiritual entities that are often conflated in modern culture:
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

为了应对即将到来的欺骗，教会必须清晰地分辨三类在现代文化中常被混淆的灵性实体：
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

### A. Holy Angels (Angels) / 圣天使（天使）
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

### A. 圣天使（天使）
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

Holy Angels are messengers of the Most High, functioning as administrative and protective agents of God’s kingdom. They are physical in their manifestations (they can eat, drink, and be touched), yet they exist beyond the constraints of our four-dimensional spacetime. They do not require a "vessel" to manifest in our realm.
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

圣天使是至高者的使者，是上帝王国的管理和保护代理人。他们在显现时具有物理性（可以吃、喝、被触摸），但他们存在于我们四维时空的限制之外。他们不需要“容器”来在我们的领域显现。
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

### B. Fallen Angels (The Watchers) / 堕落天使（守望者）
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

### B. 堕落天使（守望者）
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

Fallen Angels are those who followed Lucifer in rebellion or, specifically, the "Watchers" who "abandoned their proper abode" (Jude 1:6) to corrupt the human genome (Genesis 6). Like holy angels, they possess a high degree of physicality and advanced "supernatural technology." They are the architects of the UFO phenomenon and have the power to manifest and shape-shift without biological assistance.
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

堕落天使是那些跟随路西法背叛的实体，特别是那些“不守本位”（犹大书 1:6）去腐蚀人类基因组（创世记 6章）的“守望者”。与圣天使一样，他们拥有高度的物理性和先进的“超自然技术”。他们是 UFO 现象的设计者，拥有无需生物辅助即可显现和变形的能力。
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

### C. Demons (Disembodied Spirits of Nephilim) / 鬼魔（伟人的脱体之灵）
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

### C. 鬼魔（伟人的脱体之灵）
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

As L.A. Marzulli emphasizes, **Demons are NOT fallen angels.** According to the Book of Enoch and early church tradition, demons are the disembodied spirits of the *Nephilim*—the hybrid offspring of fallen angels and human women. Because they are the product of an illegal union between heaven and earth, they are "earth-bound" and crave a physical body to inhabit (human, animal, or object).
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

正如 L.A. Marzulli 所强调的，**鬼魔并非堕落天使。** 根据《以诺书》和早期教会传统，鬼魔是“伟人”（Nephilim）——即堕落天使与人类女子交合所生之混血后代的脱体灵魂。因为他们是天与地之间非法结合的产物，他们被“束缚于地球”，并渴望占据一个物理身体（人、动物或物体）来居住。
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

## 3. The "Gray" Avatar: The Biological Meatsuit
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

## 3. “小灰人”阿凡达：生物肉身套件
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

One of the most profound insights from Marzulli’s research is the definition of the "Grays." They are not extraterrestrial beings from another galaxy, but **biological avatars** (or "meatsuits") engineered by fallen angels for demons to inhabit.
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

Marzulli 研究中最深刻的见解之一是对“小灰人”的定义。他们不是来自另一个星系的外星生命，而是堕落天使为鬼魔设计的**生物阿凡达**（或称“肉身套件”）。
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

> "The demons are the disembodied spirits of the Nephilim. They need something to inhabit... So the enemy creates the 'Grays.' These are avatars... The demon inhabits this avatar. This is why they all look very automaton-like."
> — L.A. Marzulli
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

> “鬼魔是伟人（Nephilim）的脱体灵魂。他们需要东西来居住……所以仇敌创造了‘小灰人’。这些是阿凡达……鬼魔居住在这个阿凡达中。这就是为什么他们看起来都非常像自动机。”
> —— L.A. Marzulli
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

This explains why Grays lack reproductive organs, digestive tracts, or distinct individual personalities—they are mass-produced biological "containers" designed to facilitate the manifestation of spirits in our dimension.
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

这解释了为什么小灰人缺乏生殖器官、消化系统或独特的个体个性——他们是批量生产的生物“容器”，旨在促进邪灵在我们的维度中显现。
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

## 4. The Coming Great Deception
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

## 4. 即将到来的大欺骗
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

The ultimate goal of this disclosure is what Marzulli calls "The Coming Great Deception." When these entities officially "arrive," they will likely offer a narrative intended to collapse the Christian worldview:
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

这种揭秘的最终目标被 Marzulli 称为“即将到来的大欺骗”。当这些实体正式“降临”时，他们可能会提供一种旨在瓦解基督教世界观的叙事：
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

1.  **Direct Creation (Panspermia)**: They will claim to have "seeded" all life on Earth.
2.  **Genetic Manipulation**: They will claim to have "upgraded" early humans to create modern man.
3.  **Religious Origins**: They will claim that all world religions were started by them to guide primitive humanity.
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

1.  **直接创造（生源论）**：他们将声称“播种”了地球上的所有生命。
2.  **基因操纵**：他们将声称“升级”了早期人类以创造现代人。
3.  **宗教起源**：他们将声称世界所有的宗教都是由他们发起的，以引导原始人类。
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}

As Marzulli warns, it only takes a moment of global crisis (such as a nuclear threat) to create a "climate of fear" that will drive humanity into the arms of these "saviors."
{: .lang-en lang="en"}

正如 Marzulli 警告的那样，只需要一瞬间的全球危机（如核威胁），就能制造出一种“恐惧气候”，将人类推向这些“救世主”的怀抱。
{: .lang-zh lang="zh-CN"}
