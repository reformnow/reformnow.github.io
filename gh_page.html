The Twelve Tribes of Israel 以色列十二支派分地

How the land was divided among the sons of Israel. 应许之地在十二个支派中的行政划分。

The Promised Land

After the conquest under Joshua, the land was divided by lot among the tribes (Joshua 13–19). This map reflects the tribal allotments as described in Scripture:

  • Transjordan tribes — Reuben, Gad, and half of Manasseh received land east of the Jordan River (Numbers 32).
  • Simeon’s enclave — Simeon’s inheritance was carved out of Judah’s portion in the Negev (Joshua 19:1).
  • Philistine coast — The coastal strip from Gaza to Ekron remained unconquered during the early settlement period (Joshua 13:2-3).

这一地必是你们的

在约书亚带领征服迦南后，各支派抽签分配了土地（约书亚记13-19章）。本图反映了圣经所记载的支派分地：

  • 约旦河东支派 —— 流便、迦得和半个玛拿西支派获得了约旦河东岸的土地（民数记32章）。
  • 西缅的飞地 —— 西缅的产业是从犹大支派的南地中分出来的（约书亚记19:1）。
  • 非利士沿海 —— 从迦萨到以革伦的沿海地带，在早期定居时期仍未被征服（约书亚记13:2-3）。
