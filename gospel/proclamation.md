<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">I. The Holy Sovereign Creator</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">The Jurisprudential Foundation of Existence</span>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">一、 主权的神圣创造者</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">存在的法理根基</span>
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
God's creation was not an accidental laboratory explosion, but the manifestation of the will of a Supreme Sovereign. He is the source of all things, which means He possesses absolute, inalienable ownership and governing authority over every inch of space and every second of time.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
万有并非一次偶然的爆炸，而是一位至高主权的神的意志的彰显。祂是万有的源头，这意味着祂对每一寸空间、每一秒时间拥有绝对的、不可剥夺的拥有权与治理权。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. The Sovereignty of Being</span>
Humanity was created not for the pursuit of self-actualization, but to be a mirror of God's glory. The imprint of "Belonging to God" is etched into our created nature.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. 存在的主权</span>
人类被造，不是为了追求自我的实现，而是为了成为上帝荣耀的镜子。我们受造的本性中就刻有“属于上帝”的烙印。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. The Covenantal Beginning</span>
God placed man in a legal relationship. He is not only the Creator but also the Lord of the Covenant. This means that human happiness, peace, and life depend entirely on obedience to and union with this Sovereign.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. 圣约的开端</span>
上帝将人安置在一种法律性的关系中。祂不仅是创造者，更是立约的主。这意味着人类的幸福、平安与生命，完全取决于对这位主权者的顺服与联合。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
```bible
"Worthy are you, our Lord and God, to receive glory and honor and power, for you created all things, and by your will they existed and were created."
— Revelation 4:11
```
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
```bible
“我们的主，我们的神，你是配得荣耀、尊贵、权力的；因为你创造了万物，并且万物是因你的旨意被创造而有的。”
— 启示录 4:11
```
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>Proclamation:</strong> Your life does not belong to yourself; the legitimacy of every breath you take originates from the Holy Lord seated on the eternal throne.
</div>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>宣告：</strong> 你的生命并非属于你自己，你每一口呼吸的合法性，都源于那位坐在永恒宝座上的圣主。
</div>
</div>

---

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">II. Total Rebellion & Death</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">The Total Bankruptcy of Human Ability</span>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">二、 彻底的叛逆与死亡</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">人类能力的完全破产</span>
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
Sin is not a repairable defect; it is a forensic coup aimed at overthrowing God's throne. As the representative of all humanity, Adam's fall led to the legal bankruptcy and natural destruction of the entire human race.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
罪不是一种可以修补的缺陷，而是一场旨在推翻上帝宝座的法理政变。亚当作为全人类的代表，其堕落导致了整个人类种群在法理上的破产与本性上的灭亡。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. Total Inability</span>
Sin has not only polluted our actions but also poisoned our will. Just as a dead man cannot rescue himself, fallen humanity has completely lost the ability to desire Truth or seek God.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. 完全的无能</span>
罪不仅污染了我们的行为，更毒害了我们的意志。正如死人无法自救，堕落的人类也完全丧失了向往真理、寻求上帝的能力。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. Righteous Retribution</span>
God's holiness determines that His wrath against sin is not an emotional reaction but a necessary retribution of justice. In His courtroom, any attempt to offset sin through deeds, morality, or "decisions" is a contempt of His absolute holiness.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. 公义的追讨</span>
上帝的圣洁决定了祂对罪的烈怒不是情绪化的反应，而是公义的必然追讨。在祂的法庭上，任何试图通过行为、道德或“决志”来抵罪的企图，都是对祂绝对圣洁的藐视。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
```bible
"None is righteous, no, not one; no one understands; no one seeks for God. All have turned aside; together they have become worthless; no one does good, not even one."
— Romans 3:10-12
```
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
```bible
“没有义人，连一个也没有。没有明白的，没有寻求神的。都是偏离正路，一同变为无用。没有行善的，连一个也没有。”
— 罗马书 3:10-12
```
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>Proclamation:</strong> You not only cannot save yourself, you cannot even produce the sincerity to want to be saved. In the face of righteous judgment, our only outcome is eternal forensic death.
</div>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>宣告：</strong> 你不仅无法自救，你甚至连想要得救的诚意都无法自产。在公义的审判面前，我们唯一的结局是法理上的永恒死刑。
</div>
</div>

---

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">III. The Mediator of Grace</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">The Perfect Intercession of the Second Adam</span>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">三、 恩典中的中保</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">第二个亚当的完美顺服</span>
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
From eternity, God purposed to save His people. This salvation is not a hasty backup plan established after man sinned, but the triumph of God's sovereign decree.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
上帝在永恒中定意要救祂的百姓。这份救恩不是在人犯罪后仓促制定的补救计划，而是上帝主权旨意的凯旋。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. The Forensic Significance of the Incarnation</span>
Christ had to be fully man to represent us in fulfilling the Law; He had to be fully God for His obedience to possess infinite value.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. 道成肉身的法理意义</span>
基督必须是完全的人，才能代表我们履行律法；祂必须是完全的神，祂的顺服才具有无限的价值。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. Active Obedience</span>
Christ lived out the perfect requirements of the Law that humanity never realized. As the "Second Adam," He triumphed completely at every point of temptation. This means He earned a perfect "righteousness" that exists objectively, independent of anyone's subjective feelings.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. 主动的顺服</span>
基督活出了人类从未实现过的、完美的律法要求。祂作为“第二个亚当”，在每一个受试探的环节都完全胜出。这意味着，祂赚取了一份完美的“义”，这份义是客观存在的，不取决于任何人的主观感受。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
```bible
"For as by the one man's disobedience the many were made sinners, so by the one man's obedience the many will be made righteous."
— Romans 5:19
```
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
```bible
“因一人的悖逆，众人成为罪人；照样，因一人的顺从，众人也成为义了。”
— 罗马书 5:19
```
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>Proclamation:</strong> The hope of redemption lies not in your effort, but in the Nazarene, Jesus Christ, who lived truly in history and perfectly obeyed all of God's laws on your behalf.
</div>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>宣告：</strong> 救赎的希望不在于你的努力，而在于那位在历史中真实活过、且完美顺服了上帝一切律法的拿撒勒人耶稣。
</div>
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">IV. Substitution & Triumph</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">The Great Forensic Exchange on the Cross</span>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">四、 完美的代赎与凯旋</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">十字架上的法理交换</span>
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
The Cross was not an accidental tragedy; it was the ultimate collision of God's justice and His love. There, the greatest legally effective exchange in human history took place.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
十字架不是一场悲剧的意外，而是上帝公义与慈爱最高峰的碰撞。在那里，发生了一场人类历史上最伟大的、具有法律效力的交换。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. Particular Atonement</span>
What Christ bore on the Cross was not a vague, potentially failing punishment, but specific, real guilt. He did not die to "attempt redemption," but to "truly accomplish redemption."
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. 特定的代赎</span>
基督在十字架上所承担的，不是一种模糊的、可能失败的刑罚，而是特定的、真实的罪债。祂不是为了“尝试救赎”而死，而是为了“确实完成救赎”而死。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. Complete Victory</span>
Christ's resurrection is a "certificate of acquittal" issued by the court of Heaven. It declares that the debt of sin has been cleared, death has been abolished, and the devil has been defeated. His resurrection ensures that none who belong to Him in the covenant will ever be lost.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. 彻底的胜利</span>
基督的复活是上帝法庭下达的“无罪释放”证明书。它宣告罪债已清、死亡已废、魔鬼已败。祂的复活保证了，凡是在盟约中属于祂的人，一个都不会失丧。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
```bible
"For our sake he made him to be sin who knew no sin, so that in him we might become the righteousness of God."
— 2 Corinthians 5:21
```
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
```bible
“神使那无罪的，替我们成为罪，好叫我们在他里面成为神的义。”
— 哥林多后书 5:21
```
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>Proclamation:</strong> No unpaid bills were left on the Cross. Every drop of blood shed by Christ precisely bought the souls He intended to save, and its effect lasts unto eternity.
</div>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>宣告：</strong> 十字架上没有留下任何未付的账单。基督流出的每一滴血，都精准地买赎了祂所定意要拯救的灵魂，其效力直至永恒。
</div>
</div>

---

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">V. The Call of Sovereign Grace</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">The Grasp of Sovereign Grace</span>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">五、 恩典的呼召与信靠</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">主权恩典的抓取</span>
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
Repentance and faith are not bargaining chips produced by man to trade for salvation, but tools given by God so that salvation can be realized in man.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
悔改与信心，不是人为了换取救恩而产出的筹码，而是上帝为了让救恩落实在人身上而赐下的工具。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. Irresistible Grace</span>
When the Word is preached, the Holy Spirit works in the hearts of the elect, bringing life to the dead and sight to the blind. This call carries sovereign power—it not only issues an invitation but also creates the response.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. 不可抗拒的恩典</span>
当圣道被宣讲时，圣灵在选民心中动工，使死人复活，使瞎眼看见。这种呼召是带着主权能力的，它不仅发出了邀请，更创造了回应。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. The Nature of Faith</span>
Faith is not intellectual agreement or a leap of the will, but a wholehearted resting. It is like a trembling empty hand, discarding all self-righteousness to grasp only the righteousness of Christ. Even this "hand" is placed into our lives by God Himself.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. 信心的本质</span>
信心不是理智的赞同，也不是意志的飞跃，而是全心的安息。它像一只颤抖的空手，丢弃了所有的自以为义，仅仅抓住基督的义。连这只“手”，都是上帝亲手放在我们生命里的。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
```bible
"No one can come to me unless the Father who sent me draws him. And I will raise him up on the last day."
— John 6:44
```
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
```bible
“若不是差我来的父吸引人，就没有能到我这里来的；到我这里来的，在末日我要叫他复活。”
— 约翰福音 6:44
```
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>Proclamation:</strong> Your return was not because you were wiser or more pious than others, but because the Great Shepherd called you by your name, making it impossible for you not to follow Him.
</div>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>宣告：</strong> 你的归回不是因为你比别人更有智慧或更敬虔，而是因为那位伟大的牧人按着你的名字呼唤了你，使你不得不跟随祂。
</div>
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">VI. Covenant Peace & Assurance</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">The Unshakable Certainty of the Covenant</span>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<h2 class="section-header">六、 圣约的平安与终末</h2>
<span class="jurisdiction-title">永不摇动的确据</span>
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
The destination of salvation is not temporary peace but eternal, legally protected security.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
救恩的终点不是暂时的平安，而是永恒的、受法律保护的保障。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. The Forensic Status of Justification</span>
Our standing before God does not depend on our pious performance today, but on the unchanging righteousness of Christ. We are adopted children—a blood relationship based on a covenant, not a contract relationship based on performance.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">1. 因信称义的法理地位</span>
我们在上帝面前的地位，不取决于我们今天敬虔的表现，而取决于基督那永不改变的公义。我们是被领养的儿女，这是一种基于圣约的血缘关系，而非基于表现的合同关系。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. The Perseverance of the Saints</span>
We can persevere to the end because the God who began the good work will surely complete it. Christ's intercession ensures that our hope lies not in our grip on Him, but in His embrace of us.
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<span class="point-title">2. 圣徒的坚韧</span>
我们之所以能坚持到底，是因为那位开始动了善工的上帝必成全这工。基督的代求确保了我们的指望不在于我们对祂的抓紧，而在于祂对我们的怀抱。
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
```bible
"There is therefore now no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus."
— Romans 8:1
```
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
```bible
“如今，那些在基督耶稣里的就不定罪了。”
— 罗马书 8:1
```
</div>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>Proclamation:</strong> For those in Christ, there is now and forever no condemnation. All things may shake, but the peace granted in the covenant is as stable as the throne of God, until we see Him face to face in glory.
</div>
</div>
<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
<div class="proclamation-box">
<strong>宣告：</strong> 在基督里的人，现在及永远都不再定罪了。万物虽会震动，但那在圣约中赐下的平安，稳固如上帝的宝座，直至我们在荣耀中与祂面对面。
</div>
</div>
</div>
