    ### The Journey of Faith
    This map traces the traditional route of the Exodus, from the plains of Rameses in Egypt, across the Red Sea (likely through the Bitter Lakes or Gulf of Suez), and into the Sinai Peninsula. It marks the significant location of Mount Sinai, where the Law was given (Exodus 19-20).
  </div>

  <div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
    ### 信心之旅
    本图描绘了传统的出埃及路径，从埃及的兰塞平原出发，越过红海（很可能是经过苦水湖或苏伊士湾），进入西奈半岛。图中特别标记了颁布律法的西奈山（出埃及记 19-20章）。
